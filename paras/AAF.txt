Thomas Alva Edison, often remembered as the father of modern innovation, forever altered the course of history with his groundbreaking invention—the electric bulb. This revolutionary creation not only illuminated homes and streets but also ignited a global transformation, heralding the dawn of modernity and progress. The name Thomas Alva Edison, synonymous with brilliance and ingenuity, remains inseparable from the electric bulb, a beacon of human achievement that continues to shine brightly across generations.
